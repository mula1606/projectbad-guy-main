Throne Room

Throne room for Rameses II alias Rameses the Great
With features of lapis lazuli on the floor and the stairs.
Inspired by the story of Rameses II and Moses Represented by the Gods Seth/Set and Osiris
Set (God of chaos, desert, violence, etc) and Osiris (God of fertility, agriculture, the afterlife, the dead, resurrection, etc)
Set murdered and mutilated his brother Osiris because he was jealous of his power.
